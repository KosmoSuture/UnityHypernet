## Outreach Sequence & Timeline

### Phase 1: Preparation (Week 1)
- Finalize pitch materials
- Prepare technical requirements document
- Set up tracking system for outreach
- Customize pitch for each manufacturer

### Phase 2: Initial Contact (Weeks 2-3)
- Submit formal developer program applications (all Tier 1)
- Send personalized emails to business development contacts
- Engage on LinkedIn with key decision-makers
- Post in relevant developer communities

### Phase 3: Follow-up (Weeks 4-6)
- Follow up on applications (2-week intervals)
- Schedule calls with interested parties
- Begin Tier 2 outreach
- Provide additional materials as requested

### Phase 4: Relationship Building (Ongoing)
- Regular updates on Hypernet progress
- Engage with developer communities
- Share technical developments
- Maintain communication channels

## Success Metrics

- Number of developer program applications submitted
- Response rate from manufacturers
- Number of developer kits acquired
- Partnership discussions initiated
- Hardware donations/loans received

## Key Messages to Emphasize

1. **Open-Source Commitment** - Building in public, transparent development
2. **Universal Compatibility** - Supporting ALL VR platforms from day one
3. **Ecosystem Expansion** - Growing the total VR market benefits all manufacturers
4. **Developer-First** - Focus on developer experience and tools
5. **No Exclusivity** - Not picking winners, supporting entire industry

## Risk Mitigation

- **Low response rate:** Cast wide net, multiple contact channels
- **Competitive concerns:** Emphasize ecosystem growth, not zero-sum competition
- **Resource limitations:** Prioritize 2-3 headsets initially, expand over time
- **NDA/IP concerns:** Prepare clear IP policy, open-source licensing clarity
