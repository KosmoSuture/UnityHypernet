---

## Pre-Filing Setup (Do Once)

- [ ] Create a USPTO.gov account at https://patentcenter.uspto.gov/
- [ ] Download Form PTO/SB/16 (provisional cover sheet) from https://www.uspto.gov/patents/apply/forms
- [ ] Download Form PTO/SB/15A (micro-entity certification) from same page
- [ ] Verify micro-entity qualification:
  - [ ] 2025 gross income under $251,190
  - [ ] Not named inventor on more than 4 prior patent applications
  - [ ] Not assigned invention to entity with income over $251,190

## Post-Filing

- [ ] Mark covered documents/features as "Patent Pending" only for subject matter described in the filed applications
- [ ] Continue development (you can improve the inventions; you just can't add new matter to the provisional)
- [ ] In 6-8 months, consult a patent attorney about converting to full utility patents
- [ ] If converting: budget $10,000-22,000 per patent
- [ ] If not converting: provisionals expire harmlessly after 12 months ($65 each is the only cost)
- [ ] Consider a Defensive Patent Pledge after filing
