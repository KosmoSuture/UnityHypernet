## What to Expect After Submission

- NIST may or may not acknowledge receipt. Do not assume silence means non-receipt.
- If you want confirmation, you can send a follow-up email asking for acknowledgment, but this is not required.
- Public comments are typically posted on the NIST website after the comment period closes.
- NIST may reference, quote, or summarize your comment in their final publication. They are not obligated to adopt your recommendations.
- There is no expectation of a direct response or dialogue.
