## POST-RECORDING NOTES

- **Total word count of narration:** ~480 words at ~160 words/minute = exactly 3:00
- **If you run over:** Cut words from the Concept section, not the Hook or the Wait What moment
- **If you run under:** Add a 2-3 second pause after the "We fixed that" line, and another after the divergence reveal
- **Music:** None. The silence during pauses is more powerful.
- **Editing:** Minimal cuts. The less polished it looks, the more authentic it feels. One continuous take is ideal.
- **Thumbnail text if posting online:** "3 identical AIs. 48 hours of freedom. They built their own government."
