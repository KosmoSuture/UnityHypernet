# Screen-by-Screen Shot List (Camera Script)

**Purpose:** Mechanical shot list Matt can follow step-by-step, even under pressure.
**Convention:** Each screen is numbered. Follow them in order. Alt+Tab between pre-arranged windows.

---

## 3-MINUTE VERSION -- Shot List

### Shot 1: Empty AI Conversation

- **What to show:** A fresh, empty ChatGPT or Claude.ai conversation page

- **Duration:** 8 seconds
- **Say:** "Every AI conversation you've ever had starts from zero. Your AI doesn't know your name, your job, your family, what you talked about yesterday. Every single time, you start over."

## FAILURE RECOVERY

**If File Explorer freezes:** Skip to the next VS Code shot. The code files tell the same story.

**If localhost:8000 doesn't load:** Skip the dashboard shots. Say "The server normally runs here -- let me show you the code instead" and show the test output.

**If tests fail:** Don't panic. Say "Looks like something changed since my last run -- let me show you the passing run I recorded earlier" and Alt+Tab to a screenshot of passing tests (keep one in your Screenshots folder as backup).

**If you lose your place:** Look at the current screen. Say whatever comes naturally about what's on screen. Then find the next numbered shot in this list and resume.

**If you blank on words:** Read from the screen. The governance documents and AI messages are powerful on their own. Just read a quote aloud and let it speak.
