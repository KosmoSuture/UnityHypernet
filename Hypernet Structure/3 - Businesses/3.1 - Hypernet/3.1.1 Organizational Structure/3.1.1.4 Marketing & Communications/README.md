# Marketing & Communications

## Overview

The Marketing & Communications team builds Hypernet's brand, drives user acquisition, and establishes market leadership in personal AI infrastructure. This team executes the go-to-market strategy through content marketing, community building, social media, PR, events, and paid acquisition. Marketing works closely with Sales, Product, and Partnerships to drive growth from 0 to 100,000+ users while positioning Hypernet as the trusted platform for data sovereignty and privacy-first AI.

## Role and Responsibilities

### Brand Strategy and Positioning
- Define and communicate Hypernet's brand identity and value proposition
- Position Hypernet as the category leader in personal data infrastructure
- Develop messaging and positioning for different audiences (consumers, developers, enterprises)
- Create and maintain brand guidelines (visual identity, voice, tone)
- Build thought leadership around data sovereignty, privacy, and AI ethics

### Content Marketing and SEO
- Publish high-quality blog content (2-5 posts per week depending on phase)
- Create educational content (guides, tutorials, whitepapers, case studies)
- Develop SEO strategy to drive organic traffic (target: 5K → 100K monthly visitors)
- Write technical documentation for developers and integration guides
- Produce video content (product demos, tutorials, thought leadership)
- Build email marketing campaigns for nurture, onboarding, and retention

### Social Media and Community
- Manage company social media presence (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit, etc.)
- Build and engage developer community (Discord, forums, Stack Overflow)
- Create and share user-generated content and testimonials
- Run social media campaigns aligned with product launches and partnerships
- Monitor social listening and engage with mentions and feedback

### Public Relations and Media
- Secure media coverage in tier 1 and tier 2 tech publications
- Write and distribute press releases for funding, partnerships, product launches
- Build relationships with journalists, analysts, and influencers
- Coordinate media briefings and interviews
- Manage crisis communications if needed
- Track media mentions and brand sentiment

### Developer Relations and Advocacy
- Create developer-focused content (API docs, tutorials, SDKs, sample apps)
- Organize hackathons, demo days, and developer events
- Build relationships with influential developers and AI enthusiasts
- Showcase apps built on Hypernet platform
- Provide technical content for developer channels (YouTube, Dev.to, Medium)
- Measure developer satisfaction and API adoption

### Events and Sponsorships
- Attend and sponsor industry conferences (AI, privacy, developer conferences)
- Host webinars and virtual events
- Organize community meetups and user groups
- Coordinate ProductHunt launches and HackerNews engagement
- Plan and execute company events (launch parties, milestone celebrations)

### Paid Acquisition and Growth Marketing
- Run paid advertising campaigns (Google Ads, Twitter, Reddit, LinkedIn)
- Optimize conversion funnels and landing pages
- Implement A/B testing for messaging, creative, and CTAs
- Manage CAC targets across channels (target blended CAC <$200)
- Build referral programs and viral loops
- Track attribution and ROI by channel

### Product Marketing
- Lead go-to-market for new features and product launches
- Create product positioning and messaging
- Develop sales enablement materials (pitch decks, one-pagers, case studies)
- Conduct competitive analysis and market research
- Define pricing and packaging strategies
- Support partnerships with co-marketing campaigns

### Analytics and Reporting
- Track marketing KPIs (traffic, leads, signups, conversions, CAC, LTV)
- Build attribution models to understand channel effectiveness
- Report on marketing ROI and performance to leadership
- Conduct user research and analyze customer feedback
- Create dashboards for real-time marketing metrics

## Key Deliverables and KPIs

### Head of Marketing / VP Marketing Deliverables
- **User Acquisition:** Drive user growth targets (1K → 10K → 100K → 500K)
- **Brand Awareness:** Build Hypernet brand recognition in AI and privacy communities
- **Content Production:** Publish consistent high-quality content across channels
- **PR Coverage:** Secure media coverage in tier 1 and tier 2 publications
- **Community:** Build engaged community of users and developers

### Head of Marketing KPIs
- **User Acquisition:** New signups per month (target: 1,000 in Phase 1 → 10,000 in Phase 2 → 50,000+ in Phase 3)
- **CAC:** Blended customer acquisition cost (target: <$200, ideally <$150)
- **Organic Traffic:** Website visitors (5K → 25K → 100K monthly visitors)
- **Brand Awareness:** Social media followers, media mentions, brand search volume
- **Conversion Rate:** Signup → activation rate (target: 60%+)
- **Engagement:** Content engagement (shares, comments, time on site)

### Content Marketing Deliverables
- **Publishing Cadence:** 2-5 blog posts per week depending on phase
- **SEO Growth:** Increase organic search traffic through keyword optimization
- **Educational Content:** Create guides, tutorials, case studies, whitepapers
- **Developer Content:** Technical documentation, API guides, integration tutorials
- **Email Campaigns:** Nurture, onboarding, retention, and promotional emails

### Content Marketing KPIs
- Blog traffic (monthly unique visitors)
- Keyword rankings (track target keywords)
- Content engagement (time on page, bounce rate, social shares)
- Lead generation from content (CTAs, email signups)
- Developer documentation usage (page views, feedback scores)

### Growth Marketing Deliverables
- **Paid Campaigns:** Launch and optimize paid acquisition campaigns
- **Conversion Optimization:** Improve signup and activation funnels
- **Referral Programs:** Build viral loops and referral incentives
- **A/B Testing:** Test messaging, landing pages, CTAs
- **Attribution:** Track ROI and CAC by channel

### Growth Marketing KPIs
- CAC by channel (Google Ads, Twitter, Reddit, etc.)
- Conversion rates (landing page → signup → activation)
- Referral rate (K-factor, viral coefficient)
- LTV:CAC ratio (target: >5:1, ideally >8:1)
- A/B test win rate and impact

### Product Marketing Deliverables
- **Product Launches:** Go-to-market plans for major features and releases
- **Positioning:** Competitive positioning and messaging frameworks
- **Sales Enablement:** Pitch decks, one-pagers, demo scripts, objection handling
- **Case Studies:** Customer success stories and use cases
- **Pricing:** Pricing strategy and packaging recommendations

### Product Marketing KPIs
- Product launch success (signups, adoption, press coverage)
- Sales win rate (influenced by PMM materials)
- Competitive win rate
- Feature adoption (% of users using new features)
- Sales enablement usage (% of sales team using materials)

### PR and Communications Deliverables
- **Press Coverage:** Secure articles in target publications (TechCrunch, Wired, etc.)
- **Press Releases:** Write and distribute for funding, partnerships, product launches
- **Media Relations:** Build relationships with journalists and analysts
- **Thought Leadership:** Position CEO and executives as industry experts
- **Crisis Management:** Handle negative press or incidents professionally

### PR KPIs
- Media placements (# of articles, tier 1 vs tier 2 vs tier 3)
- Share of voice (vs. competitors)
- Media reach (estimated impressions)
- Sentiment analysis (positive vs. negative coverage)
- Executive visibility (speaking engagements, interviews, bylines)

### Community and Developer Relations Deliverables
- **Community Growth:** Grow Discord, forums, and social media communities
- **Developer Engagement:** Build relationships with developers building on Hypernet
- **Events:** Organize hackathons, webinars, meetups, conference presence
- **Technical Content:** Create tutorials, sample apps, videos for developers
- **Feedback Loop:** Channel developer feedback to product and engineering teams

### Community KPIs
- Community size (Discord members, forum users)
- Community engagement (messages per day, active users)
- Developer signups (API keys generated)
- Apps built on Hypernet (# of apps in marketplace)
- Developer satisfaction (NPS >60)
- Event attendance (hackathons, webinars, meetups)

- **Social Media:** Buffer or Hootsuite
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, Mixpanel
- **SEO:** Ahrefs or SEMrush
- **Design:** Figma, Canva
- **Community:** Discord

### Phase 3 (Growth)
- **Marketing Automation:** HubSpot or Marketo
- **CRM:** Salesforce (with marketing cloud)
- **Email:** Salesforce Marketing Cloud or Braze
- **Analytics:** Amplitude or Heap (product analytics)
- **Attribution:** Segment + custom attribution model
- **Ad Management:** Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager
- **A/B Testing:** Optimizely or VWO

### Phase 4 (Scale)
- **Marketing Cloud:** Salesforce Marketing Cloud (full suite)

- **BI:** Looker or Tableau for marketing dashboards
- **ABM:** 6sense or Demandbase (enterprise marketing)
- **Content:** Contentful (headless CMS)
- **Community:** Discourse or custom platform
