# Legal & Compliance

## Overview

The Legal & Compliance team protects Hypernet's interests, ensures regulatory compliance, and manages legal risks across all business activities. Given Hypernet's core mission of personal data sovereignty and privacy-first AI infrastructure, this team plays a critical strategic role in navigating complex privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, emerging laws), securing intellectual property, negotiating partnerships and contracts, and establishing corporate governance. Legal & Compliance is not merely a support function but a strategic enabler that allows Hypernet to operate with confidence in highly regulated domains.

## Role and Responsibilities

### Privacy Law and Data Protection
- Ensure compliance with GDPR (EU), CCPA/CPRA (California), and emerging global privacy laws
- Design privacy-by-design and privacy-by-default systems
- Draft and maintain privacy policies, terms of service, and data processing agreements
- Conduct privacy impact assessments (PIAs) for new features and integrations
- Manage data subject access requests (DSARs), deletion requests, and user rights
- Coordinate with regulators and respond to privacy inquiries
- Stay ahead of evolving privacy legislation and adapt company practices
- Train team on privacy compliance and data handling

### Contract Management
- Draft, review, and negotiate all company contracts (customer, vendor, partner, employee)
- Negotiate partnership agreements with AI platform companies (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- Review and approve enterprise customer contracts and MSAs
- Manage vendor agreements and procurement contracts
- Handle employment agreements, offer letters, and contractor agreements
- Maintain contract repository and management system
- Establish contract templates and playbooks to accelerate reviews

### Intellectual Property Protection
- Secure and maintain trademarks for Hypernet brand and products
- Evaluate and file patents for novel technology and innovations
- Protect copyrights for code, documentation, and creative works
- Manage open source licensing strategy and compliance
- Conduct IP due diligence for partnerships and acquisitions
- Defend against IP infringement and handle disputes
- Employee IP assignment agreements and invention disclosures

### Corporate Governance and Securities
- Maintain corporate records, stock ledger, and capitalization table
- Prepare and file board resolutions and corporate minutes
- Manage stock option grants and equity administration
- Coordinate 409A valuations for option pricing
- Handle securities law compliance for fundraising (Reg D, accredited investors)
- Support board meetings with legal documentation and governance
- Prepare for eventual IPO with SEC compliance and governance maturation

### Employment Law and HR Compliance
- Draft employee handbook, policies, and workplace rules
- Ensure compliance with employment laws (wage/hour, discrimination, benefits)
- Handle employee disputes, investigations, and terminations
- Review and negotiate executive compensation agreements
- Advise on HR matters (hiring, performance, equity, benefits)
- Manage employee arbitration agreements and confidentiality/non-compete clauses
- Support DE&I initiatives with legal compliance

### Regulatory Compliance and Risk Management
- Monitor regulatory landscape for data, AI, and technology companies
- Ensure compliance with industry-specific regulations (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA if applicable)
- Coordinate external audits and certifications
- Manage compliance programs and internal controls
- Respond to regulatory inquiries and government requests
- Develop incident response plans for data breaches or legal crises
- Maintain D&O insurance and other liability coverage

### Litigation and Dispute Resolution
- Manage litigation and disputes (if they arise)
- Coordinate with external litigation counsel
- Handle customer disputes and contract disagreements
- Defend against employment claims or customer lawsuits
- Manage insurance claims and risk mitigation
- Negotiate settlements when appropriate

### Mergers, Acquisitions, and Fundraising
- Support fundraising rounds with legal documentation (term sheets, stock purchase agreements)
- Conduct legal due diligence for acquisitions
- Negotiate acquisition agreements and integration
- Handle legal aspects of M&A transactions
- Coordinate with outside counsel on complex deals

### AI Ethics and Emerging Technology Law
- Navigate evolving legal landscape for AI regulation (EU AI Act, etc.)
- Advise on ethical AI development and deployment
- Ensure AI features comply with anti-discrimination and fairness requirements
- Monitor AI liability and accountability legal developments
- Contribute to AI policy discussions and advocacy

## Key Legal Processes and Policies

### Contract Review Process
1. **Intake:** Business team submits contract via legal request system
2. **Triage:** Legal ops triages and assigns to appropriate counsel
3. **Review:** Counsel reviews and provides redlines/comments
4. **Negotiation:** Counsel negotiates terms with counterparty
5. **Approval:** Counsel approves and routes for signature
6. **Execution:** Business team executes via DocuSign or other system
7. **Repository:** Executed contract stored in CLM system

**SLAs:**
- Standard contracts (using template): 3 business days
- Non-standard contracts: 7 business days
- Complex/high-value contracts: 14 business days

### Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) Process
1. **Trigger:** Product/engineering proposes new feature or integration
2. **Assessment:** Privacy counsel reviews feature for privacy implications
3. **Documentation:** PIA document completed with risk assessment
4. **Mitigation:** Privacy risks identified and mitigation plan created
5. **Approval:** Privacy counsel approves or requires changes
6. **Monitoring:** Ongoing monitoring of privacy compliance post-launch

### Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) Process
1. **Receipt:** User submits DSAR via email or portal
2. **Verification:** Verify identity of requestor
3. **Gathering:** Collect all personal data about user from systems
4. **Review:** Review data for legal holds or exemptions
5. **Response:** Provide data to user within 30 days (GDPR)
6. **Documentation:** Log DSAR and response for audit trail

### Stock Option Grant Process
1. **Approval:** Compensation committee or board approves grants
2. **409A Valuation:** Ensure current 409A valuation for strike price
3. **Grant Agreement:** Prepare stock option grant agreement
4. **Delivery:** Deliver to employee via equity management system (Carta, etc.)
5. **Administration:** Record grant in cap table and stock ledger

### Corporate Governance Calendar
- **Quarterly:** Board meetings with full legal documentation (minutes, resolutions)
- **Annual:** Annual stockholder meeting, 409A valuation, corporate filings
- **Ongoing:** Stock option grants, corporate record maintenance

## Privacy and Data Protection Strategy

### Privacy by Design
- Privacy considerations integrated into product development from day one
- Privacy impact assessments required for all new features
- Data minimization: collect only necessary data
- User consent and control: explicit consent, easy opt-out, data deletion

### Privacy by Default
- Strictest privacy settings enabled by default
- User data encrypted in transit and at rest
- Access controls and least privilege principles
- Optional end-to-end encryption and zero-knowledge architecture (Phase 3+)

### User Rights and Transparency
- Clear, readable privacy policy (not just legal boilerplate)
- Easy-to-use privacy controls in user dashboard
- Transparent about data usage, sharing, and retention
- Simple process for users to access, correct, or delete their data

### International Data Transfers
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers
- Adequacy decisions where available
- Potential EU data residency option (Phase 3+)
- Compliance with emerging international data transfer regulations

### Data Retention and Deletion
- Clear data retention policies aligned with business needs and legal requirements
- Automated data deletion when retention period expires
- User-initiated deletion honored promptly
- Soft delete with recovery period, then hard delete

## Intellectual Property Strategy

### Trademarks
- **Phase 1:** File "Hypernet" trademark (word mark and logo) in US
- **Phase 2:** File in key international markets (EU, UK, Canada, Australia)
- **Phase 3:** File additional marks for product names and slogans
- **Ongoing:** Monitor for infringement, renew registrations

### Patents
- **Phase 1-2:** Evaluate patentability of core innovations
- **Phase 3:** File provisional patents for key technology (privacy architecture, AI agent framework)
- **Phase 4:** Develop patent portfolio for defensibility and licensing
- **Strategy:** Focus on patents that differentiate Hypernet, not obvious/incremental

### Copyrights
- Automatic copyright protection for code, documentation, content
- Ensure all employee and contractor agreements assign IP to company
- Copyright notices on all published materials
- DMCA compliance for user-generated content (if applicable)

### Trade Secrets
- Protect proprietary algorithms, processes, and business strategies as trade secrets
- Confidentiality agreements with employees, contractors, partners
- Access controls and need-to-know policies for sensitive information
- Trade secret audit and inventory

### Open Source Strategy
- SDKs and developer tools: Permissive licenses (Apache 2.0, MIT)
- Core platform (if open sourced): Copyleft license (AGPL) to prevent cloud competitors
- Contribution guidelines and CLAs for community contributions
- Open source compliance: track and audit all open source usage
