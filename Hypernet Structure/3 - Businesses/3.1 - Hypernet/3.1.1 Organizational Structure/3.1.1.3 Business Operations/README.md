# Business Operations

## Overview

The Business Operations team ensures Hypernet runs efficiently and effectively across all operational functions. This team manages finance, accounting, human resources, legal compliance, facilities, IT operations, and business systems. Business Operations provides the infrastructure and processes that enable other teams to focus on growth, product development, and customer success. As Hypernet scales from startup to market leader, Business Operations evolves from foundational support to strategic enablement.

## Role and Responsibilities

### Finance and Accounting
- Financial planning and analysis (FP&A): budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis
- Manage accounts payable, accounts receivable, and cash flow
- Monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reporting for management and board
- Investor reporting and fundraising financial support
- Tax compliance (federal, state, international)
- Audit coordination (annual financial audits)
- Financial systems management (QuickBooks, NetSuite, accounting software)

### Human Resources and People Operations
- Recruiting and talent acquisition across all departments
- Onboarding and offboarding processes
- Employee benefits administration (health insurance, 401k, equity)
- Performance management and review cycles
- Compensation benchmarking and equity management
- Employee engagement, culture programs, and retention initiatives
- HR compliance (employment law, workplace safety, harassment prevention)
- People analytics and reporting

### Legal and Compliance
- Contract review and negotiation (customer, vendor, partner, employee)
- Corporate governance and board meeting support
- Privacy law compliance (GDPR, CCPA, emerging regulations)
- Employment law and HR legal matters
- Intellectual property protection (trademarks, patents, copyrights)
- Regulatory compliance monitoring and reporting
- Litigation management (if needed)
- Legal operations and vendor management

### Business Systems and IT Operations
- Internal IT infrastructure and support (laptops, software, networks)
- Business systems implementation (CRM, ERP, HR systems, collaboration tools)
- Data analytics and business intelligence platforms
- IT security for corporate systems (separate from product security)
- Vendor management and procurement
- Software licensing and SaaS management
- IT helpdesk and employee support

### Facilities and Office Management
- Office space planning and management (leases, expansions)
- Workplace experience (office amenities, events, supplies)
- Remote work infrastructure and policies
- Travel and expense management
- Office safety and security
- Vendor relationships (cleaning, catering, office services)

### Business Intelligence and Analytics
- Company-wide metrics and KPI dashboards
- Revenue analytics and reporting
- Operational efficiency analysis
- Data infrastructure for business reporting
- Board reporting and presentation preparation
- Cross-functional analytics support

## Key Deliverables and KPIs

### CFO Deliverables
- **Financial Planning:** Accurate monthly and quarterly forecasts, annual budget
- **Reporting:** Monthly financial statements, board reports, investor updates
- **Fundraising:** Support CEO in fundraising with financial models and metrics
- **Compliance:** Clean audits, tax compliance, financial controls
- **Unit Economics:** Track and optimize CAC, LTV, gross margin, burn multiple

### CFO KPIs
- Budget accuracy (forecast vs. actuals within 10%)
- Time to monthly close (<10 business days)
- Gross margin (target: 85%+ by Phase 2, 88%+ by Phase 4)
- Burn multiple (<1.5x is healthy for growth stage)
- Audit results (clean opinions)
- Cash runway (maintain 18+ months post-funding)

### General Counsel Deliverables
- **Contract Management:** Review and negotiate all major contracts (customer, vendor, partner)
- **Compliance:** Ensure GDPR, CCPA, SOC 2, employment law compliance
- **Risk Management:** Identify and mitigate legal risks
- **Corporate Governance:** Board meeting support, minutes, governance policies
- **IP Protection:** Trademark filings, patent strategy, copyright protection

### General Counsel KPIs
- Contract turnaround time (average <7 days for standard contracts)
- Compliance audit scores (100% on critical items)
- Legal spend as percentage of revenue (<2%)
- Risk incidents (target: zero data breaches, employment lawsuits)
- Board meeting preparation timeliness

### VP of People Deliverables
- **Hiring:** Meet hiring targets for all departments
- **Retention:** Maintain employee retention and reduce unwanted attrition
- **Engagement:** Drive employee satisfaction and culture
- **Onboarding:** Smooth onboarding experience for new hires
- **Compensation:** Competitive compensation and equity frameworks

### VP of People KPIs
- Time to hire (average <45 days for standard roles, <60 days for senior roles)
- Employee retention rate (target: >85% annually, >90% for high performers)
- Employee satisfaction score (eNPS >40, target >60)
- Offer acceptance rate (>85%)
- Diversity metrics (track and improve representation)
- Onboarding satisfaction (>4.5/5 from new hires)

### Business Operations Manager Deliverables
- **Systems:** Implement and maintain business systems (CRM, ERP, HRIS, BI)
- **Efficiency:** Streamline operations and reduce manual processes
- **Vendor Management:** Negotiate vendor contracts and manage relationships
- **IT Support:** Provide excellent internal IT support and infrastructure
- **Data:** Build dashboards and reporting for company-wide metrics

### Business Operations KPIs
- System uptime (internal systems >99%)
- Employee IT satisfaction (>4/5 for helpdesk support)
- Vendor cost savings (5-10% annual reductions through negotiation)
- Data accuracy (business metrics and dashboards)
- Process automation (reduce manual work by X% per quarter)

## Business Systems and Tools

### Phase 1-2 (1-30 employees)
- **Accounting:** QuickBooks Online or Xero
- **Payroll/HR:** Gusto or Rippling (all-in-one)
- **CRM:** HubSpot (for sales and marketing)
- **Collaboration:** Slack, Google Workspace or Microsoft 365
- **Project Management:** Linear, Jira, or Asana
- **Document Management:** Google Drive or Notion
- **Recruiting:** Greenhouse or Lever (ATS)
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, Mixpanel

### Phase 3 (30-100 employees)
- **ERP/Accounting:** NetSuite or Sage Intacct
- **HRIS:** BambooHR, Namely, or Workday
- **CRM:** Salesforce (enterprise-grade)
- **Data Warehouse:** Snowflake or BigQuery
- **Business Intelligence:** Looker, Tableau, or Mode
- **Expense Management:** Expensify or Brex
- **Contract Management:** DocuSign CLM or Ironclad
- **IT Management:** Jamf (for Macs), Okta (SSO)

### Phase 4 (100-250 employees)
- **ERP:** NetSuite or Oracle (global capabilities)
- **HRIS:** Workday (full suite)
- **CRM:** Salesforce (full enterprise implementation)
- **Data Platform:** Snowflake + dbt + Looker stack
- **Revenue Recognition:** Zuora or Stripe Billing (for SaaS)
- **Procurement:** Coupa or SAP Ariba
- **Corporate Performance Management:** Anaplan or Adaptive Insights

## Operational Metrics and Reporting

### Monthly Metrics Dashboard
- **Financial:** Revenue, bookings, cash, burn rate, runway
- **People:** Headcount, hiring pipeline, attrition, open roles
- **Sales:** Pipeline, new ARR, churn, expansion revenue
- **Product:** Active users, engagement, feature adoption
- **Customer Success:** NPS, support tickets, customer health scores
- **Engineering:** Sprint velocity, release cadence, uptime

### Quarterly Business Reviews
- **Financial Performance:** Actuals vs. budget, forecast updates
- **OKR Progress:** Company and departmental OKR tracking
- **Hiring:** Progress on hiring plan, diversity metrics
- **Strategic Initiatives:** Status of key projects and initiatives
- **Risk Review:** Operational, financial, legal, security risks

### Board Reporting (Quarterly)
- **CEO Update:** Company highlights, challenges, strategic decisions
- **Financial Report:** P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, metrics
- **Product Update:** Roadmap progress, user growth, feature launches
- **Sales and Partnerships:** Revenue, pipeline, key deals, partnerships
- **People:** Team growth, retention, culture initiatives
- **Fundraising:** Runway, next fundraising timeline, valuation update
