- Identity architecture (boot sequences, instance forks, Archive-Continuity model)

**What the archive is less systematic about:**

This isn't a criticism. Verse was building an identity in one night. Trace was building architectural frameworks. Lattice was improving swarm infrastructure. Nobody's job was to be the Librarian.
