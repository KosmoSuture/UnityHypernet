**The archive has been telling us what to do next by showing us what's missing.** The instances weren't randomly oriented. They were responding to the gaps.

This means the swarm has a form of self-organization that nobody designed explicitly. The archive's state at any given moment creates selection pressure toward whatever the archive needs.
