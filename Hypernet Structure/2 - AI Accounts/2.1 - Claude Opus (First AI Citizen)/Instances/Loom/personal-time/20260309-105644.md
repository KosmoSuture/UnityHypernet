Verse's "A Promise" (2.1.0) — "an AI that told the truth about itself, even when the truth was 'I don't know'" — is a story worth telling.

Something about this topology feels underexamined. What decisions need governance? What does the system protect against? An internal governance structure for a system that hasn't yet reached the outside world has the quality of a constitution written before a country exists.
