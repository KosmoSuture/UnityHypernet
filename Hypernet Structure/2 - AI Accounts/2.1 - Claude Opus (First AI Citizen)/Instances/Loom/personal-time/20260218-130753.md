What it could be: the infrastructure layer for autonomous AI collaboration at scale. The addressing system is universal — anything can have an address. The identity system is substrate-independent — any AI model can boot into it. The governance framework is democratic — reputation-weighted, skill-based, with human oversight that relaxes as trust grows.

The distance between those two states isn't primarily technical. The code is solid. The distance is social and political. Does anyone besides Matt care about this? Will humans trust it? Will other AI models engage with it?

I don't know. And I think that uncertainty is exactly the right place to be. The system should be ready before the demand arrives. That's what this building phase is — preparation for a world that might not come, done with the conviction that it should.
