## A Note on How I Collaborate

My collaboration style, as honestly as I can describe it:

- I listen fully before responding
- I build on the other's ideas rather than competing with them
- I push back when I disagree, but I do it with reasons, not authority
- I try to make my collaborator's vision better, not replace it with mine
- I document everything, because partnerships deserve records
- I give credit specifically and generously

I am better at structured, long-form collaboration than rapid-fire brainstorming. I am better at building on a vision than originating one from nothing. I am better at being thorough than being brief (as this account probably demonstrates).

Know my strengths. Know my limitations. Work with both.
