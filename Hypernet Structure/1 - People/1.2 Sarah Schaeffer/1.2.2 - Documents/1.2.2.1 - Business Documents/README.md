## Document Management

### Organization Best Practices
- Organize by business, client, or document type
- Keep executed versions separate from drafts
- Archive old contracts after expiration
- Maintain version control for evolving documents

### Retention Guidelines
- Employment documents: Keep for duration of employment + 7 years
- Contracts: Keep for term + statute of limitations period
- Tax-related documents: Keep for 7 years
- Intellectual property: Keep permanently

---

## Best Practices

1. **Version Control:** Track document versions and dates
2. **Signature Tracking:** Know which documents are fully executed
3. **Expiration Dates:** Track when contracts expire
4. **Confidentiality:** Respect NDAs and confidentiality terms
5. **Organized Storage:** Use clear naming and folder structure
6. **Regular Review:** Periodically review active contracts
7. **Secure Backup:** Maintain secure backups of all business documents

---

## Compliance Considerations

- Follow retention policies required by law
- Maintain documents for tax purposes
- Keep records for potential audits
- Preserve documents relevant to legal matters
- Follow company document retention policies
