## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Highly Private:** Most documents contain sensitive information
- **Confidential:** Legal and financial documents require strict protection
- **Selective Sharing:** Only share specific documents when necessary
- **Encrypted Storage:** Consider encryption for sensitive documents

### Data Sensitivity
- Personal identification documents are extremely sensitive
- Financial records must be protected
- Legal documents often contain confidential information
- Medical records are protected by privacy laws
- Business documents may have proprietary information

### Sharing Controls
- Documents should only be shared with explicit permission
- Track who has accessed which documents
- Set expiration dates on shared access
- Maintain audit logs of document access

## Best Practices

1. **Organize Clearly:** Use consistent naming and folder structure
2. **Back Up Regularly:** Keep multiple copies of important documents
3. **Scan Physical Docs:** Digitize important paper documents
4. **Version Control:** Keep track of document versions
5. **Secure Storage:** Protect sensitive documents appropriately
6. **Regular Review:** Periodically review and organize documents
7. **Expiration Tracking:** Note when documents expire or need renewal
