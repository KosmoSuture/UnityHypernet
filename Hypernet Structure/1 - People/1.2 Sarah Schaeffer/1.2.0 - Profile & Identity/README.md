## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Private:** Most identity information is private by default
- **Professional:** Work history and skills may be shared selectively
- **Public:** Limited information for public profiles only
- **Family:** Shared with family members only

### Data Sensitivity
- Contact information is highly sensitive
- Work history may be semi-public for networking
- Personal attributes should remain private
- Education history varies by context

### Sharing Controls
- Identity information is tightly controlled
- Selective sharing for specific purposes
- Regular review of what information is public
- Easy revocation of shared access
