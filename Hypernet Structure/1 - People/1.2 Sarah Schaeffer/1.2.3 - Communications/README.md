## What Goes Here

### Communication Types
- **Email:** Important email threads and archives
- **Meeting Notes:** Minutes, agendas, action items
- **Letters:** Formal correspondence and letters
- **Messages:** Saved conversations and messages
- **Call Notes:** Notes from phone calls and video calls
- **Discussion Threads:** Important online discussions

## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Private:** Most communications are private
- **Professional:** Work communications may be less sensitive
- **Confidential:** Some correspondence is highly confidential

### Data Sensitivity
- Email contains personal and private information
- Meeting notes may include sensitive discussions
- Correspondence may be confidential
- Professional communications may be proprietary

---

## Best Practices

1. **Archive Important Emails:** Save crucial communications
2. **Take Good Notes:** Document key decisions and action items
3. **Organize by Context:** Group related communications together
4. **Date Everything:** Always include dates
5. **Search Optimization:** Use clear, searchable names
6. **Regular Cleanup:** Remove unnecessary communications
7. **Respect Privacy:** Maintain confidentiality
