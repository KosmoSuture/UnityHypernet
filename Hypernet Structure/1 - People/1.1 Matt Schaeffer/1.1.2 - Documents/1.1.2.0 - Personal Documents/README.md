- **Family Sharing:** Some documents may be shared with family

## Best Practices

1. **Write Regularly:** Consistent journaling builds valuable history
2. **Date Everything:** Always include dates on personal documents
3. **Be Honest:** These are for you - be truthful
4. **Backup Carefully:** Multiple secure backups recommended
5. **Think Long-term:** These become family history
6. **Organize by Date:** Chronological organization often best
7. **Tag Key Moments:** Mark significant life events
8. **Include Context:** Future you will want details
9. **Preserve Originals:** Keep physical originals of special items
10. **Review Annually:** Reread and reflect on past entries
