## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Professional:** Shareable within professional context
- **Confidential:** Client documents under NDA
- **Internal Only:** Company-specific information
- **Public:** Marketing materials and presentations

### Data Sensitivity
- **High Sensitivity:**
  - Contracts with financial terms
  - NDAs and confidential agreements
  - Salary and compensation details
  - Proprietary business information
  - Client confidential data

- **Medium Sensitivity:**
  - Business proposals and quotes
  - Meeting minutes
  - Internal reports
  - Vendor agreements

- **Low Sensitivity:**
  - Public presentations
  - Marketing materials
  - General correspondence

### Legal Considerations
- Maintain contract retention requirements
- Preserve audit trail for financial documents
- Respect NDA obligations
- Follow data retention policies
- Consider statute of limitations
- Keep records required by law (typically 7 years)

## Document Retention Guidelines

### Permanent Retention
- Articles of incorporation
- Partnership agreements
- Major contracts (keep permanently)
- Intellectual property documents
- Business formation documents

### 7+ Years
- Tax-related documents
- Financial statements
- Audit reports
- Employment records
- Payroll records

### 3-7 Years
- General contracts (after expiration)
- Vendor agreements
- Client contracts (after completion)
- Project documentation

### 1-3 Years
- General correspondence
- Routine reports
- Meeting minutes
- Proposals (won or lost)

## Best Practices

1. **Sign and Date:** Ensure all agreements are properly executed
2. **Version Control:** Track document versions and revisions
3. **Naming Convention:** Use clear, consistent file names
4. **Organize by Entity:** Separate by company if multiple businesses
5. **Backup Critical Docs:** Extra backups of important contracts
6. **Track Deadlines:** Note renewal dates, termination clauses
7. **Secure Storage:** Encrypt sensitive business documents
8. **Regular Audits:** Review and organize quarterly
9. **Index Key Terms:** Note key dates, values, parties
10. **Link Related Items:** Connect contracts to projects/invoices

---

## Compliance & Legal

### Best Practices for Legal Protection
- Never alter signed documents
- Maintain original signed copies
- Track amendment history
- Note counter-parties and signatories
- Record execution dates
- Store in secure, accessible location
- Follow retention requirements
- Prepare for audits

### If Legal Issues Arise
- Business documents are evidence
- Preserve all related documents
- Don't delete or alter anything
- Consult with attorney about discovery
- Maintain chain of custody
