## Purpose

This folder archives completed tasks, providing a historical record of what you've accomplished and when. This archive helps with productivity tracking, performance reviews, and maintaining a sense of achievement.

---

## What Goes Here

### Completed Task Types
- **Finished Work Tasks:** Completed job assignments and deliverables
- **Completed Projects Tasks:** Finished project-related tasks
- **Done Personal Tasks:** Completed personal to-dos and errands
- **Accomplished Action Items:** Finished meeting action items
- **Achieved Goals:** Completed goal-related tasks
- **Resolved Issues:** Fixed problems and resolved tickets
- **Delivered Outcomes:** Completed deliverables and milestones

### Task Completion Information
- Original task description
- Completion date and time
- Time spent (estimated vs actual)
- Who completed it
- Outcome and results
- Quality of completion
- Lessons learned
- Related deliverables
- Notes on how it went

## Examples of What Might Be Stored

### Completed Work Task
```markdown
# Q1 Financial Report - COMPLETED

**Completed:** February 14, 2026
**Original Due Date:** February 15, 2026
**Priority:** High
**Project:** Q1 Business Review

## Task Description
Compile and analyze Q1 financial data, create presentation slides, and prepare executive summary.

## Time Tracking
- Estimated Time: 4 hours
- Actual Time: 5.5 hours
- Difference: +1.5 hours (better to track all details)

## Outcome
Successfully completed comprehensive Q1 financial report. Presentation delivered to executive team on 2/14. Well-received, sparked good strategic discussion.

## Deliverables
- PowerPoint presentation (28 slides)
- Executive summary (3 pages)
- Supporting financial spreadsheets
- Year-over-year analysis charts

## Lessons Learned
- Start data gathering earlier (waiting on accounting delayed start)
- Create template for future quarters
- Visualizations were very effective - invest more time in charts
- Allow buffer time before deadline

## Related
- Presentation file: /reports/q1-2026-financial-report.pptx
- Next: Q2 report due May 15, 2026
```

## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Private:** Completed tasks remain private
- **Shareable:** Some accomplishments can be shared
- **Portfolio:** Major achievements may be public
- **Performance Reviews:** Work tasks used for reviews

### Data Sensitivity
- Completed tasks may contain:
  - Proprietary work information
  - Client confidential data
  - Personal health information
  - Financial details
  - Sensitive personal matters

### Security
- Archive tasks retain original privacy level
- Work tasks subject to company retention policies
- Personal tasks kept as long as useful
- Sensitive completed tasks need continued protection

## Retention & Archival

### Keep Indefinitely
- Major accomplishments and milestones
- Career-defining tasks and projects
- Significant personal achievements
- Tasks with lasting impact
- Reference examples for future work

### Keep 3-7 Years
- Routine work tasks (for performance reviews)
- Project-related tasks (project lifecycle)
- Client deliverables (contract period + time)
- Financial tasks (tax period)

### Keep 1 Year
- Daily/weekly routine tasks
- Small personal tasks
- Quick admin tasks
- Minimal impact tasks

### Can Archive/Delete
- Trivial completed tasks
- Redundant or duplicate tasks
- No historical value
- No reference needed

## Best Practices

1. **Mark Completion Date:** Always record when completed
2. **Note Outcome:** What was the result?
3. **Track Time:** Actual time helps future estimates
4. **Add Lessons:** What did you learn?
5. **Link Deliverables:** Connect to outputs produced
6. **Celebrate Wins:** Acknowledge accomplishments
7. **Review Regularly:** Weekly/monthly reflection
8. **Extract Insights:** Learn from completion patterns
9. **Update Skills:** Note new abilities developed
10. **Share Success:** Communicate achievements appropriately
