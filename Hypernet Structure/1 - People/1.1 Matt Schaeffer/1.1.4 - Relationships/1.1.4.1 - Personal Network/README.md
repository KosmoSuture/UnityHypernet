## Purpose

This folder stores information about personal relationships including family, friends, social connections, and community relationships.

---

## What Goes Here

### Personal Relationship Types
- **Family:**
  - Immediate family (parents, siblings, children, spouse/partner)
  - Extended family (aunts, uncles, cousins, grandparents)
  - In-laws and step-family
  - Family friends

- **Friends:**
  - Best friends and close friends
  - Good friends
  - Casual friends and acquaintances
  - Childhood friends
  - College/school friends
  - Neighborhood friends
  - Activity/hobby friends

- **Social Connections:**
  - Social media friends
  - Online community connections
  - Gaming friends
  - Hobby group members
  - Club members

- **Community:**
  - Neighbors
  - Community group members
  - Volunteer organization contacts
  - Religious community members
  - Parents of kids' friends

### Relationship Information
- Full name and nickname
- Relationship type and context
- Contact information
- Important dates (birthday, anniversary)
- Family connections
- Shared history and memories
- Interests and hobbies
- Communication preferences
- Gift ideas and preferences
- Important life events
- Last contact date

## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Highly Private:** Personal relationship information is very private
- **Family Only:** Some info may be shared within family
- **Confidential:** Personal details stay confidential
- **Respect Privacy:** Don't share others' information

### Data Sensitivity
- **Extremely Sensitive:**
  - Personal contact information
  - Family issues and conflicts
  - Health information
  - Financial situations
  - Relationship problems
  - Personal struggles
  - Candid assessments

### Ethical Considerations
- Keep personal information secure
- Don't share without permission
- Respect people's privacy
- Update information with consent
- Honor communication preferences
- Maintain confidentiality of conversations
- Protect children's information especially
