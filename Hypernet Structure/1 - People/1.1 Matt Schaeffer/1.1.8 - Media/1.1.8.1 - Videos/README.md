## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Private:** Most personal videos are private
- **Family:** Family videos shared with family
- **Selected Sharing:** Choose who sees what
- **Public:** Rarely - only specific videos

### Video Privacy Concerns
- **Children's Videos:**
  - Extra privacy protection needed
  - Avoid public posting
  - Consider children's future privacy
  - Limit identifying information

- **Personal Moments:**
  - Home videos stay private
  - Intimate family moments protected
  - Medical/sensitive content secured

- **Location Security:**
  - Disable GPS on public videos
  - Don't reveal home address
  - Be cautious with security cameras
  - Travel videos post after returning

## Video Storage & Backup

### Storage Requirements
- **4K Video:** ~350 MB per minute
- **1080p Video:** ~130 MB per minute
- **720p Video:** ~60 MB per minute

### Backup Strategy
**3-2-1 Rule for Videos:**
- 3 copies of videos
- 2 different storage types
- 1 offsite/cloud backup

**Implementation:**
- Original: Phone/camera
- Cloud: iCloud/Google Photos
- Local: External hard drive (2-4TB)
- Archive: Hypernet storage

### File Formats
- **MP4:** Most common, widely compatible
- **MOV:** Apple standard, high quality
- **HEVC/H.265:** High compression, newer
- **AVI:** Older format, large files
- **MKV:** Flexible container format
- **WebM:** Web-optimized format

## Technical Considerations

### Video Quality
- **4K:** Best quality, large files
- **1080p:** Great quality, reasonable size
- **720p:** Good quality, smaller files
- Choose based on device, storage, use case

### Frame Rates
- **24fps:** Cinematic look
- **30fps:** Standard video
- **60fps:** Smooth motion, sports
- **120fps+:** Slow motion capability

### Audio Quality
- Use external mic when possible
- Avoid windy conditions
- Check audio levels
- Add music in editing
- Clean up background noise

## Best Practices

1. **Backup Immediately:** Don't risk losing videos
2. **Film Horizontally:** For better viewing experience
3. **Stabilize:** Use stabilization features
4. **Good Lighting:** Lighting makes huge difference
5. **Short Clips:** Easier to organize and edit
6. **Capture Audio:** Good audio is critical
7. **Edit Highlights:** Create shorter edited versions
8. **Organize Promptly:** Tag and describe right away
9. **Share Appropriately:** Consider privacy
10. **Preserve Originals:** Keep unedited versions
