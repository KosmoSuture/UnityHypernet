## What Goes Here

### Photo Types
- **Family Photos:** Family members, gatherings, celebrations
- **Event Photos:** Parties, weddings, birthdays, holidays

- **Screenshots:** Screen captures from devices
- **Scanned Photos:** Digitized old physical photos
- **Artistic Photography:** Creative and artistic shots
- **Nature & Landscape:** Scenic photography
- **Pet Photos:** Pictures of pets
- **Food Photos:** Meals and culinary moments

### Photo Metadata
- **EXIF Data:**
  - Date and time taken

  - Location (GPS coordinates)
  - Camera settings (ISO, aperture, shutter speed)
  - Resolution and file size

- **Hypernet Enrichment:**
  - People in photo (face recognition)
  - Objects detected
  - Scene description
  - Location name
  - Event association
  - Albums and collections
  - Tags and keywords
  - Favorites marking
  - Editing history

## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Private:** Personal and family photos default to private
- **Family:** Family photos shared with family members
- **Friends:** Social photos shared with friends
- **Public:** Selected photos made public (social media, portfolio)

### Photo Privacy Settings
- **Private Photos:**
  - Personal moments
  - Family intimate moments
  - Unedited/casual photos
  - Children's photos (extra protection)
  - Medical/sensitive images

- **Shared Photos:**
  - Event photos (parties, gatherings)
  - Travel photos (shareable)
  - Public social media posts
  - Professional headshots

### Children's Photos
- Special privacy protection
- Limit sharing publicly
- Avoid identifying information
- Get permission when older
- Consider their future privacy

### Facial Recognition
- Face detection to tag people
- Privacy controls on face data
- Opt-out available
- Never shared without permission
- Used only for organization

## Best Practices

1. **Backup Regularly:** Don't lose precious memories
2. **Organize as You Go:** Tag and album photos promptly
3. **Delete Bad Photos:** Don't keep blurry/duplicate shots
4. **Protect Privacy:** Be thoughtful about sharing
5. **Tell Stories:** Add captions and context
6. **Print Important Photos:** Physical backups matter
7. **Create Albums:** Organize by events and trips
8. **Share with Family:** Let family enjoy photos too
