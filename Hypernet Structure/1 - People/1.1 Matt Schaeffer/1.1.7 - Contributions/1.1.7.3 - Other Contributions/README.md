## Best Practices

### Community Participation
1. **Be Helpful:** Genuinely try to help
2. **Be Patient:** Explain clearly and kindly
3. **Be Thorough:** Provide complete answers
4. **Be Professional:** Maintain professionalism
5. **Give Context:** Explain the "why"
6. **Stay Current:** Keep knowledge up to date
7. **Follow Up:** Check if answer helped
8. **Be Humble:** Admit when you don't know

### Speaking & Teaching
1. **Know Your Audience:** Tailor content appropriately
2. **Prepare Thoroughly:** Practice and refine
3. **Use Examples:** Real-world demonstrations
4. **Encourage Questions:** Make it interactive
5. **Share Resources:** Provide useful links
6. **Be Authentic:** Share real experiences
7. **Time Management:** Respect time limits
8. **Follow Up:** Provide contact for questions

### Mentorship
1. **Listen First:** Understand their goals
2. **Set Expectations:** Clear understanding of relationship
3. **Be Consistent:** Regular meetings
4. **Give Honest Feedback:** Constructive and kind
5. **Share Experiences:** Real stories help
6. **Make Introductions:** Leverage your network
7. **Celebrate Wins:** Acknowledge progress
8. **Let Them Lead:** Support their goals, not yours
