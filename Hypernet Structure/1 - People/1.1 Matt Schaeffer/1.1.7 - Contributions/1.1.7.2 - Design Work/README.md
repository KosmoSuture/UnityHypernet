## What Goes Here

### Design Types
- **UI/UX Design:** User interface and experience design
- **Graphic Design:** Visual graphics, logos, branding
- **Web Design:** Website layouts and designs
- **Product Design:** Product mockups and prototypes
- **Logo Design:** Brand identity and logos
- **Icon Design:** Icon sets and individual icons
- **Typography:** Font choices and text styling
- **Color Schemes:** Palette design and color theory
- **Wireframes:** Lo-fi structural layouts
- **Mockups:** Hi-fi visual designs
- **Prototypes:** Interactive design prototypes
- **Design Systems:** Component libraries and style guides
- **Marketing Design:** Promotional graphics, ads, social media
- **Presentation Design:** Slide decks and presentations
- **Print Design:** Printable materials (if applicable)

## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Portfolio Work:** Public showcase (with permission)
- **Client Work:** May be confidential until launch
- **Personal Projects:** Public
- **Proprietary:** Company work may be NDA-protected

### Design Privacy
- **Public Portfolio:**
  - Personal projects
  - Published client work (with permission)
  - Case studies
  - Open source design

- **Confidential:**
  - Pre-launch client work
  - Proprietary company designs
  - NDA-protected projects
  - Unreleased products

- **Portfolio Consideration:**
  - Get client permission before showcasing
  - Redact sensitive information
  - Wait until product launches
  - Respect NDAs and contracts
