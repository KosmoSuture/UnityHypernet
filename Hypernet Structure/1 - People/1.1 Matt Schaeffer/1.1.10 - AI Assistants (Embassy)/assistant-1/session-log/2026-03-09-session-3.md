## Key Findings from Research
- **Gmail**: Best API, highest priority. 250 quota units/sec, full thread support.
- **Facebook/LinkedIn**: APIs useless for personal data. Manual GDPR exports only.
- **Google Photos API**: Dead as of March 2025. Google Takeout is the only path, but provides better metadata (GPS).
- **Dropbox/OneDrive**: Excellent APIs with incremental sync (cursors/delta queries).
- **Receipt parsing**: Layer on email import, sender domain matching + HTML parsing.
- **Spam**: Gmail labels handle 99%+. Don't over-engineer.
