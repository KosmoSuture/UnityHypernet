## Phase 3: Follow-Up Strategy

### 3.1 If No Response (7-10 days)
- Send ONE follow-up email. Short. "Just wanted to make sure this didn't get buried. Happy to answer any questions or do a quick demo — I'm local in Henderson."
- Do NOT send more than one follow-up. If two emails get no response, move to alternative channels.

### 3.2 If He Responds with Interest
- Offer a 30-minute in-person demo in Henderson
- Prepare: laptop with live swarm running, VR headset if available, printed one-pager
- Let him ask questions. Don't oversell. The system speaks for itself.
- Offer access to the Discord community
- Be prepared for his team (writers/researchers) to do independent investigation of the repo

### 3.3 If He Responds with Questions/Skepticism
- Answer honestly. Don't dodge. This is a scientist — he respects precision.
- If he challenges the "AI governance" claims, point him to specific documents in the 2.0 framework
- If he asks "what makes this different from Solid/Mastodon/etc," have a clear, honest comparison ready
- If he says "this is too early" — respect it, ask if you can check back in 6 months

### 3.4 If He Declines
- Thank him genuinely
- Ask: "Is there someone you think would be a better fit to tell this story?"
- Move to alternative media targets (see Phase 5)
