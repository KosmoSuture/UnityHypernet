## Purpose

This folder stores daily notes, personal thoughts, quick captures, and ongoing note-taking that doesn't fit into more specific categories.

---

## What Goes Here

### Note Types
- **Daily Notes:** Daily journaling and logging
- **Quick Captures:** Ideas, thoughts, observations captured quickly
- **Scratch Notes:** Temporary working notes
- **Random Thoughts:** Stream of consciousness, brainstorming
- **Reflection Notes:** Personal reflections and insights
- **Decision Logs:** Decision-making process notes
- **Dream Journal:** Dreams remembered
- **Gratitude Journal:** Things you're grateful for
- **Morning Pages:** Free-form morning writing
- **Evening Reflection:** End-of-day thoughts
- **Life Events:** Notable moments and experiences
- **Mood Tracking:** Emotional state notes
- **Personal Development:** Self-improvement notes
- **Goal Tracking:** Progress toward personal goals

### Note Metadata
- Date created
- Date modified
- Tags and keywords
- Related notes (links)
- Category or type
- Status (draft, final, archived)
- Word count
- Mood/sentiment (optional)

## Privacy Considerations

### Default Privacy Level
- **Highly Private:** Personal notes are deeply private
- **For Your Eyes Only:** Default is private
- **Selective Sharing:** Rarely shared, if ever
- **Legacy:** May designate some for posthumous sharing

### Content Sensitivity
Personal notes often contain:
- Raw, unfiltered thoughts
- Private emotions and struggles
- Relationship dynamics
- Financial concerns
- Health anxieties
- Career doubts
- Personal failures and fears
- Dreams and aspirations
- Controversial opinions

### Security Measures
- Encryption recommended
- Strong access controls
- No AI access (unless you choose)
- Separate backup strategy
- Clear legacy instructions
- Password protected

## Best Practices

1. **Write Daily:** Consistency builds habit
2. **Lower the Bar:** Doesn't have to be perfect
3. **Be Honest:** Notes are for you, be truthful
4. **Date Everything:** Always include date
5. **Quick Capture:** Write ideas immediately
6. **Review Regularly:** Re-read past notes
7. **No Judgment:** Don't criticize your writing
8. **Keep Private:** Protect your privacy
9. **Backup Carefully:** Don't lose years of notes
10. **Enjoy Process:** Writing should be satisfying

## Digital vs Analog

### Digital Notes Advantages
- Searchable
- Backed up automatically
- Accessible anywhere
- Easy to organize
- Can include media
- Link between notes

### Handwritten Notes Advantages
- Better for memory
- More creative freedom
- No distractions
- Personal and tactile
- Some find more meaningful
- Can't be hacked/lost if digital fails

### Hybrid Approach
- Handwrite daily, scan/photograph
- Digital for searchable archive
- Analog for deep thinking
- Digital for quick capture
- Best of both worlds
