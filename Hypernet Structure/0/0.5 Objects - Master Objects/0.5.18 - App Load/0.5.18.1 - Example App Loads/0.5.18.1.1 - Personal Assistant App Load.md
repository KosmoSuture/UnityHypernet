## Status Notes

- Draft only. No official hash or attestation exists yet.
- The app code does not exist yet.
- Calendar write, meeting mode, email, and always-listening are
  intentionally outside the MVP default grant.
- Any future private locker access must become a new app-load
  revision or a narrowly scoped mandala grant.
