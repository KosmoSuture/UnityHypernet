# 0.6.1.8 - related_to

**Category:** Person Relationship (0.6.1)
**Directed:** No | **Symmetric:** Yes | **Transitive:** No

## Description

Family relationship. Indicates biological, legal, or adoptive kinship between two people. Has subtypes for specific relationships: parent_of, child_of, sibling_of, spouse_of, grandparent_of.

## Properties

- **relationship_type**: parent | child | sibling | spouse | cousin | etc.
- **biological**: Whether the relationship is biological
- **legal**: Whether the relationship is legally recognized

## Verification

Document verification (birth certificate, marriage certificate, adoption papers) or mutual confirmation.
