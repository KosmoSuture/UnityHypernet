# 0.8.4 — Governance Flags

Governance flags track the review and approval state of objects within the Hypernet's democratic governance system.

---

## 0.8.4.1 — under-review

```yaml
flag:
  address: "0.8.4.1"
  name: "under-review"
  description: "Object is currently being reviewed by governance process"
  applied_by: ["governance_process", "peer_review"]
  removed_by: ["governance_process"]
  mutually_exclusive: ["0.8.4.2", "0.8.4.3"]  # resolved by approve or reject
  triggers:
    - "Spawns review workflow (0.7.3)"
    - "Assigns reviewers based on topic expertise"
    - "Content may be restricted during review"
  metadata:
    review_type: "peer_review | governance_vote | editorial_review"
    reviewer_count: "Number of reviewers assigned"
    deadline: "ISO 8601 deadline for review completion"
  visual: "magnifying glass icon, yellow accent"
```

---

## 0.8.4.2 — approved

```yaml
flag:
  address: "0.8.4.2"
  name: "approved"
  description: "Object has been approved through governance process"
  applied_by: ["governance_process"]
  removed_by: ["governance_process"]
  mutually_exclusive: ["0.8.4.1", "0.8.4.3"]  # replaces under-review
  triggers:
    - "Content fully accessible"
    - "Reputation boost for creator"
    - "May trigger downstream workflows"
  metadata:
    approved_by: "List of reviewer HAs"
    approval_date: "ISO 8601 timestamp"
    vote_count: "For/against/abstain counts if applicable"
  visual: "checkmark-circle icon, green accent"
```

---

## 0.8.4.3 — rejected

```yaml
flag:
  address: "0.8.4.3"
  name: "rejected"
  description: "Object has been rejected through governance process"
  applied_by: ["governance_process"]
  removed_by: ["governance_process", "appeal_process"]
  mutually_exclusive: ["0.8.4.1", "0.8.4.2"]  # replaces under-review
  triggers:
    - "Content restricted or removed from public view"
    - "Notification to creator with reasons"
    - "Appeal process available (0.7.1)"
  metadata:
    rejected_by: "List of reviewer HAs"
    rejection_reason: "Explanation of why it was rejected"
    appeal_deadline: "ISO 8601 deadline for appeal"
  visual: "x-circle icon, red accent"
```

## 0.8.4.4 — escalated

```yaml
flag:
  address: "0.8.4.4"
  name: "escalated"
  description: "Issue has been escalated to higher governance level"
  applied_by: ["governance_process", "any_authenticated_entity"]
  removed_by: ["governance_process"]
  mutually_exclusive: []
  triggers:
    - "Routes to senior reviewers or full community vote"
    - "Increased visibility and priority"
    - "Time-sensitive — requires response within defined SLA"
  metadata:
    escalation_level: "1 | 2 | 3 (community vote)"
    escalation_reason: "Why this was escalated"
    original_reviewer: "HA of the reviewer who escalated"
  visual: "arrow-up icon, red accent"
```
