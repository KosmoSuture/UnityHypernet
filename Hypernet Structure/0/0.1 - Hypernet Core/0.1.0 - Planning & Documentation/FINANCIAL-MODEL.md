## Revenue Model

### B2B API Revenue (70% of total)

**Pricing Structure:**

| Data Type | Price per API Call | Avg Calls/User/Month | Monthly Rev per User |
|-----------|-------------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Basic (Calendar, Tasks) | $0.01 | 1,000 | $10 |
| Standard (Email, Contacts) | $0.03 | 500 | $15 |
| Premium (Social, Photos) | $0.05 | 300 | $15 |
| Advanced (Health, Finance) | $0.10 | 100 | $10 |
| **Total per User** | - | **1,900** | **$50** |

**Enterprise Customer Profile:**
- Average customer: 800 users
- Monthly revenue per enterprise: $40,000
- Annual contract value (ACV): $480,000
- Gross margin: 85%

**Customer Acquisition:**
- Sales cycle: 3-6 months
- Close rate: 25% (qualified leads)
- Sales rep capacity: 8 deals/year at full productivity
- Rep ramp time: 6 months

### B2C Premium Revenue (25% of total)

**Pricing Tiers:**

| Tier | Price/Month | Features | Target Market | Est. % |
|------|-------------|----------|---------------|--------|
| Free | $0 | 5 connections, basic features | Mass market | 85% |
| Pro | $19 | Unlimited connections, AI features | Power users | 12% |
| Premium | $49 | Everything + API access, priority | Developers | 3% |

**Conversion Funnel:**
- Free users: 85% of total
- Convert to Pro: 12% (14% of free users)
- Convert to Premium: 3% (25% of Pro users)
- Weighted ARPU: $4.50/month = $54/year

### Data Licensing Revenue (5% of total)

**Structure:**
- Aggregated, anonymized data only
- Opt-in required from users
- Enterprise research contracts
- Average deal size: $500K/year
- Target customers: 10-20 by Year 5

## Unit Economics

### B2B Enterprise Customers

**Customer Acquisition:**
- Lead generation cost: $2,000 per qualified lead
- Sales rep cost: $200K fully loaded
- Rep capacity: 8 deals/year (at maturity)
- Close rate: 25%
- CAC: $25,000 per customer

**Customer Lifetime Value:**
- ACV: $480,000
- Gross margin: 85%
- Retention: 95% annual (very sticky)
- Lifetime: 5+ years
- LTV: $1.9M (conservative 4-year calc)

**LTV:CAC Ratio:** 76:1 (exceptional)
**Payback Period:** 0.6 months (very fast)

**Reality Check:** Even at 50% lower margins and 2x higher CAC, we're at 19:1 LTV:CAC - still excellent.

### B2C Premium Users

**Customer Acquisition:**
- Organic (70%): $50 CAC (content, SEO, viral)
- Paid (20%): $400 CAC (ads, influencers)
- Partnerships (10%): $0 CAC (distributed by B2B customers)
- Blended CAC: $110

**Customer Lifetime Value:**
- Monthly ARPU: $20 (blended Pro + Premium)
- Gross margin: 90%
- Retention: 75% annual (25% churn)
- Lifetime: 2.5 years average
- LTV: $540

**LTV:CAC Ratio:** 4.9:1 (healthy for B2C)
**Payback Period:** 6 months

**Improving over time:**
- Year 1: LTV:CAC = 3:1 (investing in growth)
- Year 3: LTV:CAC = 6:1 (hitting efficiency)
- Year 5: LTV:CAC = 8:1 (mature, optimized)

## Risk Factors & Mitigations

### Revenue Risks

**Risk: Slower enterprise adoption**
- Mitigation: Focus on partnerships for distribution
- Mitigation: Build strong ROI case studies
- Impact: Delay to profitability by 6-12 months

**Risk: Lower B2C conversion**
- Mitigation: Improve product value, reduce friction
- Mitigation: Focus on B2B2C model (partners drive users)
- Impact: 20-30% revenue reduction

**Risk: Pricing pressure**
- Mitigation: Build strong moat, differentiate on privacy
- Mitigation: Enterprise value pricing, not commodity
- Impact: 10-20% margin compression

### Expense Risks

**Risk: Higher personnel costs**
- Mitigation: Remote-first reduces costs
- Mitigation: Equity-heavy compensation
- Impact: 10-15% higher burn

**Risk: Slower hiring**
- Mitigation: Contract/fractional resources
- Mitigation: Prioritize key hires (CTO first)
- Impact: Delayed milestones but lower burn

**Risk: Infrastructure costs higher**
- Mitigation: Multi-cloud strategy
- Mitigation: On-premise option for enterprise
- Impact: 5-10% margin impact

### Market Risks

**Risk: Big Tech competitive response**
- Mitigation: They can't - conflict of interest
- Mitigation: Partner with them instead
- Impact: Market validation, actually helps us

**Risk: Regulatory changes**
- Mitigation: Work with regulators proactively
- Mitigation: Over-invest in compliance
- Impact: Could be positive (mandates our solution)

**Risk: AI market slowdown**
- Mitigation: Diversify use cases beyond AI
- Mitigation: Enterprise data value even without AI
- Impact: 30-50% slower growth
