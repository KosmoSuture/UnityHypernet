## Risk Mitigation

### Major Risks & Responses

**Risk 1: Can't raise from angels**
- **Mitigation:** Apply to YC, Techstars, or other accelerators ($150K-500K + network)
- **Mitigation:** Bootstrap longer, focus on revenue-first approach
- **Mitigation:** Seek non-traditional investors (revenue-based financing, venture debt)

**Risk 2: VCs don't see the vision**
- **Mitigation:** Pivot pitch to different angle (B2B infrastructure vs. consumer)
- **Mitigation:** Focus on corporate strategics who feel the pain directly
- **Mitigation:** Prove with revenue and traction, not just vision

**Risk 3: Big Tech announces competing solution**
- **Mitigation:** Move fast, build moat through partnerships
- **Mitigation:** Position as neutral, privacy-first alternative (Big Tech can't claim this)
- **Mitigation:** Europe-first strategy (EU won't trust Big Tech with this)

**Risk 4: Regulatory challenges**
- **Mitigation:** Hire privacy counsel early
- **Mitigation:** Work WITH regulators (EU, FTC) to shape standards
- **Mitigation:** Position as solution TO regulatory requirements (GDPR compliance)

**Risk 5: Market timing wrong**
- **Mitigation:** Multiple pivots possible (pure AI infrastructure, enterprise only, etc.)
- **Mitigation:** Strong technical foundation allows adaptation
- **Mitigation:** Grants provide non-dilutive runway to wait for market
