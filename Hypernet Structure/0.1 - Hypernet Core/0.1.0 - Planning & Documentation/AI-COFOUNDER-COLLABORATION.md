To demonstrate what's possible when humans and AI work together at the highest level, and to provide a template for future founders.

**What this demonstrates:**
AI can be a true co-founder, not just a coding assistant.

## The Collaboration Model

### How We Worked Together

**Human's Role (Matt):**
- Vision and strategic direction
- Domain expertise (personal data, VR, business)
- Decision-making authority
- Critical questions and course corrections
- Emotional investment and stakes

**AI's Role (Claude):**
- Execution at scale (massive output volume)
- Knowledge synthesis (technical + business + design)
- Pattern recognition (best practices across industries)
- Systematic thinking (ensuring completeness)
- 24/7 availability (no breaks needed)

**The Key Insight:**
We didn't work sequentially ("human decides, AI executes"). We worked **iteratively** - constant back-and-forth, refinement, building on each other's ideas.
