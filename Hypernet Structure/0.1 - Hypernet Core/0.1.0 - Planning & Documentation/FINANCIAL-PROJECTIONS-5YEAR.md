## Revenue Streams (4 Models)

### Stream 1: Consumer Subscription

**Pricing tiers:**
- **Free:** 10GB storage, basic features, ad-supported
- **Premium:** $9.99/month - Unlimited storage, VR features, AI marketplace access
- **Family:** $24.99/month - Up to 6 members, shared features
- **Lifetime:** $299 one-time (promotional, limited)

**Assumptions:**
- 10% free â†’ premium conversion (industry standard)
- 20% premium â†’ family upgrade
- Average LTV: $500 over 4 years

### Stream 2: AI Data Marketplace

**How it works:**
- AI companies pay users $5-50/month for data access
- Hypernet takes 30% platform fee
- Users keep 70%

**Example per user:**
- OpenAI: $10/month for conversation context
- Anthropic: $8/month for document access
- Google: $12/month for photo metadata
- User receives: $21/month
- Hypernet receives: $9/month (30% of $30)

**Assumptions:**
- 50% of premium users opt into AI marketplace
- Average AI company payment: $30/month total
- Hypernet cut: $9/month per participating user

### Stream 3: Enterprise (B2B)

**Target:** Companies needing employee data management

**Pricing:**
- **SMB:** $50/user/year (10-100 employees)
- **Mid-market:** $100/user/year (100-1,000 employees)
- **Enterprise:** $200/user/year (1,000+ employees)

**Features:**
- Unified employee data management
- GDPR/compliance tools
- Company-wide search
- Admin controls
- SSO integration

**Assumptions:**
- Launch Year 2
- 5% of user base converts to enterprise licenses

### Stream 4: Platform/Developer Fees

**Marketplace for third-party apps:**
- Hypernet takes 15-30% of app sales (like App Store)
- API access fees for developers
- Data export services

**Assumptions:**
- Launch Year 3
- 10% of revenue in Year 5
