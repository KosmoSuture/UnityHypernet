## Production Steps

### Step 1: Generate Individual Scenes (Day 1-2)

For each scene (19 total):

1. **Go to Runway ML** (runwayml.com)
2. **Click "Gen-3 Alpha"**
3. **Paste the "AI Video Prompt"** from each scene
4. **Add motion control:**
   - Scene 1-3: "Slow rotation, dramatic"
   - Scene 9: "Camera flying forward, fast"
   - Scene 10-11: "Smooth gliding motion"
   - Scene 16: "Epic zoom out, accelerating"
5. **Generate** (takes 1-2 minutes per scene)
6. **Download** as MP4
7. **Repeat** for all 19 scenes

**Time:** 4-6 hours (lots of waiting)
**Cost:** ~$5-10

### Step 2: Generate Voiceover (Day 2)

1. **Go to ElevenLabs** (elevenlabs.io)
2. **Choose voice:** "Josh" (professional male) or "Bella" (warm female)
3. **Paste entire voiceover script**
4. **Add timing markers:**
   ```
   [pause 3s]
   Your life is scattered
   [pause 3s]
   across fifty different apps.
   [pause 4s]
   etc.
   ```
5. **Generate**
6. **Download** as MP3

**Time:** 30 minutes
**Cost:** ~$5

### Step 3: Find Music (Day 2)

**Recommended tracks** (royalty-free):

### Step 4: Edit Together (Day 3)

**Use: DaVinci Resolve** (FREE) or Adobe Premiere Pro

1. **Create new project:** 1920x1080, 30fps
2. **Import all:**
   - 19 video clips
   - 1 voiceover file
   - 1 music track
3. **Timeline:**
   - Music track (full length, fade in/out)
   - Video clips (arrange per storyboard)
   - Voiceover track (synced to video)
4. **Transitions:**
   - Quick cuts for Act 1-2 (energy)
   - Smooth dissolves for Act 3 (wonder)
   - Fade to black for Act 5 (clean)
5. **Color grade:**
   - Slightly desaturated for realism
   - Boost contrast
   - Add slight glow to VR scenes
6. **Sound mix:**
   - Music: -12db (background)
   - Voice: -3db (clear, prominent)
   - Add sound effects (optional):
     - Whoosh for transitions
     - UI beeps for interfaces
     - Ambient hum for VR scenes
7. **Export:**
   - H.264, 1920x1080, 30fps
   - High quality (8-10 Mbps)
   - "Hypernet_Demo_v1.mp4"

**Time:** 4-6 hours editing
**Cost:** $0 (DaVinci Resolve is free)

### Step 5: Add Text/Graphics (Day 3)

**Create in Canva or After Effects:**

1. **Hypernet logo animation** (Scene 18)
2. **Text overlays** (Scene 19):

3. **Export** as PNG sequence or MOV with alpha
4. **Import to edit**
5. **Place over video**

**Time:** 2 hours
**Cost:** $0 (Canva free tier)
